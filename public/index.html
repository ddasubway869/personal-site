Something new

We're experimenting.

ARVL is still young, and album discovery is still the whole point. But we're trying some things.

The Shelf Save albums to your Collection or Listen Later list. Build a personal shelf of what you've heard and what's next.
Comments & Likes Leave notes on any album. Like what other members are saying. It's small, but it's something.
Follow & Feed Follow other members and see what they're picking, collecting, and listening to in the Community feed.
Discussion A board for all of it — feature requests, questions, sharing taste, or just saying hi.
Notifications Get notified when someone likes your picks, comments, or replies.
Genre Tags Albums now have genre tags. The first member to pick or tag an album sets it for everyone. New picks get tagged in the flow — and if you've already picked something untagged, you can add it from the album page.

We're figuring this out as we go. Come experiment with us.

About ARVL

Like many people, I got tired of algorithms telling me what to listen to. Discover Weekly gives you songs. The charts give you whatever's trending. None of it felt personal.

So I built ARVL. One album a week, picked by a real person in the community. Just people who actually listen to albums, front to back, recommending what they're genuinely into.

Built by an album listener, for album listeners.

Thank you for being here — DeAndre

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

What we collect
When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and a hashed (encrypted) version of your password. We also store the album picks, ratings, and comments you submit.

What we don't collect
We don't collect your Spotify account data. Spotify is used only to search and display album information — no connection to your personal Spotify account is required or stored.

How we use your data
Your email is used only to verify your account. We don't send marketing emails, and we never sell or share your data with third parties.

Data storage
Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

Deleting your account
Want your data removed? Email us at hello@arvl.app and we'll delete your account and associated data.
