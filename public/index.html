About ARVL

I built ARVL because I was tired of algorithms telling me what to listen to.

Discover Weekly gives you songs. The charts give you whatever's trending. None of it felt personal.

ARVL is different. One album a week, picked by a real person in the community. Just people who actually listen to albums — front to back — recommending what they're genuinely into.

Built by an album listener, for album listeners.
